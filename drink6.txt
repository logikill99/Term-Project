A True Amaretto Sour
Amaretto;Lemon;Ice;Maraschino cherry;
Make in a Old-fashioned glass then Rub the rim of an old fashioned glass with lemon, and dip repeatedly into granulated sugar until it has a good "frosted" rim. Shake a jigger of Amaretto with the juice of 1/2 a lemon. Strain into glass and add ice. Garnish with a Marachino Cherry.
