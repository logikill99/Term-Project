Hot Toddy
Whiskey;Honey;Cinnamon;lemon;Cloves;
Make in a Irish coffee cup then STEP 1Whisk the whisky and honey together and split between 2 heatproof glasses. Add half of the cinnamon stick to each, then top up with 200ml boiling water.STEP 2Add a splash of lemon juice to each, then taste and add more to your preference. Finish each with a slice of lemon, studded with a clove, and serve immediately.
