Gin Lemon
Gin;Lemon Juice;Lemon Peel;Ice;
Make in a Highball glass then For the preparation of the gin lemon you will not need the shaker. Fill the tumbler with ice, pour the gin and lemonade over it. Gently mix and decorate with a slice of lemon. Those who prefer can also add a few mint leaves. Your gin lemon is ready to be served.
