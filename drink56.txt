Dirty Martini
Vodka;Dry Vermouth;Olive Brine;Lemon;Olive;
Make in a Cocktail glass then Pour the vodka, dry vermouth and olive brine into a cocktail shaker with a handful of ice and shake well.Rub the rim of a martini glass with the wedge of lemon.Strain the contents of the cocktail shaker into the glass and add the olive.A dirty Martini contains a splash of olive brine or olive juice and is typically garnished with an olive.
