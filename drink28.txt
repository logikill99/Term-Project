Arizona Twister
Vodka;Malibu rum;Gold tequila;Orange juice;Pineapple juice;Cream of coconut;Grenadine;Ice;Pineapple;
Make in a Hurricane glass then Just mix in the shots of rum, vodka, and tequila. Add splashes of the three juices, heavy on the pineapple. Top off with grenadine. Crushed ice should already be in glass. Top off the glass with a pineapple wedge.
